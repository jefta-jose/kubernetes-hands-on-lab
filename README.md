# Kubernetes Concepts Hands-On Lab

This repository is a beginner-friendly, hands-on lab for learning Kubernetes concepts.

When you join a team, you are not usually expected to write everything from scratch or immediately deploy large, complex workloads. These labs reflect that experience by giving you partially completed Kubernetes resources with missing parameters. Each lab includes guidance to help you understand what to insert and why.

You can use the labs in whichever way best supports your learning:

- Fill in the missing values by following the provided guidance.
- Research and complete the missing pieces on your own.
- Rewrite the entire resource from scratch when you are ready for an extra challenge.

Every lab also has a dedicated solutions branch named <folder>-solution . After attempting a lab, you can check its solutions branch and compare it with your work. Try solving the exercise yourself first—the solutions are most useful as a learning and comparison tool.
